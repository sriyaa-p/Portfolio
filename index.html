Ice Cream Project

Challenge Statement

Before the ice cream melted, I had to design something fun, colorful, and impossible to ignore. Brain freeze was real.Before they ran out of sprinkles, waffle cones, and questionable flavor combinations, this project came to life. Think pastel scoops, extra toppings, and design decisions made under the influence of sugar. Somewhere between strawberry swirls and minty confusion, a cute, playful layout was born. It’s fun, it’s sweet, and it absolutely does not take itself too seriously. An additional scoop was added for no real reason.
